# Code of Conduct

ClawBuddy follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and patient.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good intent and help others learn.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussions welcoming for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Avoid harassment, insults, discrimination, or sustained disruptive behavior.

## Reporting

If conduct in this project becomes unsafe or unwelcoming, contact the repository maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or contributors to keep the project healthy.
